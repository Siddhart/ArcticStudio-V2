Turnip notifyer

Description:

Notify other people in other servers about your turnip prices.

Commands:

Here you can find a table with all my commands.

Command (without the '()') What does it do?
!ac help Get all the commands
!ac sell (dm/DodoCode) (price) Let people know that they can sell turnips on your island.
!ac buy (dm/DodoCode) (price) Let people know that they can buy turnips on your island.
!ac setup Setup the channels that the bot needs.
!ac reset Use this command after you deleted the channels i made.
