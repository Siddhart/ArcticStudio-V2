ACNH Villager Wiki

Description:

Get info about your favourite villagers in acnh. I will remind you of the villagers that have their birthday today.

Commands:

Here you can find a table with all my commands.

Command What does it do?
ac help Get all the commands
ac today Get the villagers that have their birthdays today.
ac who (month/day) Get the villager that has their birthday on the given date.
ac setup Type this command in the channel where you want the bot to send the notifications.
ac stop Type this command if you want the bot to stop sending messages in the assigned channel.
