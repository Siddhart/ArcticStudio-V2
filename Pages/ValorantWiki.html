Valorant Wiki

Description:

The Valorant Wiki can give you information about your favourite agents, weapons, maps and much more.

Commands:

Here you can find a table with all my commands.

Command What does it do?
V agents Type this to get all the names of the agents.
V agent {AGENT_NUMBER} Get more info about a agent.
V random Get a random agent to play with.
V weapons Type this to get all the names of the weapons.
V weapon {WEAPON_NUMBER} Get more info about a weapon.
V maps Type this to get all the names of the maps.
V map {MAP_NUMBER} Get more info about a map.
