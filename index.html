About Us

Hey, Welcome to our official website.

We are Arctic Studio. Arctic Studio is a small development studio with talented people.

Our team exists of artists, programmers and people with other skills.

Check out our published projects here.


Arctic Studio was created in 2018 by two friends that met each other trough a game jam.

Arctic Studio was originally a game development studio. We now also work on other projects such as websites and discord bots.

Our discord bots are being used by 1.000.000+ users.

We now have an amazing community with 500+ people and its expanding every day!


You can also join our official Discord Server here.

Almost all the projects we work on are for non-profit. If you like our projects and wish to support us you can, by supporting us on Ko-fi. You can also donate us via PayPal.
