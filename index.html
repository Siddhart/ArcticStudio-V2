About Us

Hey, Welcome to our official website.

We are Arctic Studio. Arctic Studio is a development studio with talented people.

Our team exists of artists, programmers and people with other skills.

Check out our projects here.


Arctic Studio was created in 2018 by two friends that met each other trough a game jam.

The studio name was first used to publish games under. Later it started to expand and we also work on other projects.

We now have a amazing community and its expanding every day!


You can join our Discord Server here.

Most of the projects we work on are non-profit. If you like our projects and wish to support us you can, by supporting us on Ko-fi. or via PayPal.
